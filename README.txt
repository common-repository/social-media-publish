Automatically publishing of your posts to Social Media plattforms.

== Description ==

This Plugin automatizate your Social Media processes, by publishing your posts to Social Media plattforms.

= A quick look into Social Media Publish: =

* **Auto Publish** - to Instagram
* **Auto Publish** - to Facebook
* **Reupload** - your post manually
